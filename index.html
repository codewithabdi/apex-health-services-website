FAQ

Quick & Clear Answers

Common questions about Apex Health Services and how our support process works.

What services does Apex Health Services provide?

Apex Health Services provides community-based support including personal care, community integration, respite care, and health and wellness support.

How do I get started with services?

You can contact us by phone, email, or the contact form. Our team will listen to your needs and explain the next steps.

Do you support individuals and families in Columbus, Ohio?

Yes. Apex Health Services is based in Columbus, Ohio and supports individuals and families in the local community.

Can services be personalized?

Yes. Support is based on each person’s needs, goals, routine, and level of independence.

How can families contact Apex Health Services?

Families can call us at (480) 842-9492 or email apexhealthservicesoh@gmail.com.

What makes Apex different?

Apex focuses on compassionate care, clear communication, community connection, and support that respects each person’s dignity and independence.
